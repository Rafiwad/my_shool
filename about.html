Our History

Nalerigu Senior High School is located in the northern region in the East Mamprusi district of Ghana. It is a mixed school that offers General Science, General Arts, Agriculture, Business, and Home Economics. It mostly draws students from the West Mamprugu area schools and the far east.

GREAT Nalerigu Senior High School was founded in the 1950s, first started as an Agricultural college.

The school was finally established in 1972, as known today, after going through some cycles of academic statuses as an educational institution. The school has grown from a small, innovative, and nurturing school in the heart of Nalerigu to a vibrant, diverse, and inclusive institution that values social equity and the environment.

In 2019, the school was recognized as the 20th-ranked school in Ghana by the Ghana Education Service (GES).

Since its establishment, GREAT Nalerigu Senior High School has churned out very influential, prolific, and industrious alumni who have served and are currently serving in government, academia, and the private sector, contributing immensely to national development.

The likes of Hon. Inusah Fuseini, who has served in various capacities in Government and Parliament, and Professor Kwara Nantomah, Dean of Mathematical Sciences at C.K Tedam University of Sciences in Navrongo, are proud products of the school.

GREAT Nalerigu Senior High School is committed to creating a school environment that values diversity, respects differences, and encourages empathy and understanding while maintaining a strong commitment to social equity and the environment.

We will also continue to work towards our mission and vision, ensuring that our students, staff, and parents feel valued and supported throughout their education.

VISION

ALUMNI

Hon. Inusah Fuseini

Inusah Abdulai Bistav Fuseini is a Ghanaian lawyer and politician. He is a former member of parliament for Tamale Central constituency and the former Minister for Lands and Natural Resources, former Minister of Roads and Highways in the Ghana government. He was the senior associate with the Law Trust Company in Accra before he went into politics.

Professor Kwara Nantomah

A Full Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Mathematics, C.K. Tedam University of Technology and Applied Sciences. He is currently the Dean of School of Mathematical Sciences. His research interest is in Mathematical Analysis with a particular interest in Mathematical Inequalities and Special Functions. His current project is "Inequalities for Special Functions related to the Gamma function".
