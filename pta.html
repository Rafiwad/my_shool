PTA - Parent Teacher Association

PTA (Parent Teacher Association) is a non-profit organization that aims to provide support, education, and resources to parents and their children. The organization is committed to promoting a strong and inclusive environment for all students, regardless of their socioeconomic background, gender, and disability status.

At Great Nalerigu Senior High School, PTA plays a crucial role in fostering a welcoming and inclusive school environment. Through various programs and events, PTA members work to help students develop their skills, interests, and abilities, and to better understand their families and communities.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The aims and objectives of the Association are:

  • To support, advise, and assist the Board of Directors to maintain and improve the educational standards of the School.
  • To provide additional equipment and facilities in the School.
  • To ensure the goodwill and cooperation among parents.
