Welcome to Great NASS

Discover a World of Possibilities at Great Nalerigu Senior High School, we offer a rigorous academic program, a wide range of extracurricular activities, and a supportive community that empowers students to reach their potential. Our dedicated staff are committed to providing a comprehensive education that fosters academic excellence, character development, and a lifelong love of learning.

We invite you to join us in exploring the world of Great Nalerigu Senior High School. Let's get started!

OUR MISSION AND VISION

MISSION

To inspire and empower students to reach their full potential, by providing academic excellence, character development, and a lifelong love of learning.

VISION

To be the most prestigious and influential institution in the region, where students can achieve their full potential and contribute to the world of education.

GOALS

To create a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for students, with a strong focus on academic excellence, character development, and a lifelong love of learning.
